Trustees, Governors & Reports

Responsible for the governance of this wonderful institution

Annual Reports

Our annual reports can be found on the website of the Charity Commission for England and Wales. You can view reports dating back to the financial year ending in 2010.

To view an annual report, visit http://www.charity-commission.gov.uk

  • Search for the charity using the registered charity number, 246460, in the purple search box
  • Click on "View accounts" located on the left hand navigation bar of the window
  • You now have access to the annual report PDF files
