Useful Links

Notable organisations that can provide further support and information

  • Blesma | The Limbless Veterans: Blesma supports servicemen who have lost limbs, use of limbs & eyesight through combat and after service.
  • Combat Stress | The Veterans Mental Health Charity: At Combat Stress we treat Veterans suffering from PTSD Depression and Anxiety.
  • Forces Pension Society: Not-for-profit organisation fighting and campaigning for Service pensions rights for the armed forces and their families.
  • Haig Housing: Housing assistance throughout the UK for ex-Service people.
  • Merchant Navy Welfare Board: Promotes the highest quality of welfare services to merchant seafarers and their dependants in the UK.
  • Royal Airforce Benevolent Fund: The leading welfare charity for the RAF. We are the heart of the RAF family.
  • Royal Airforces Association: Providing welfare support for Serving and Ex-Serving RAF personnel and their families.
  • Royal Naval Association: A family of current and former Naval Service personnel, relatives and supporters of our country's Royal Navy.
  • Seafarers UK (Formerly KGFS): Providing funding to organisations and projects that support seafarers and their families in need.
  • SSAFA | The Armed Forces Charity: The charity that provides lifelong support for our Forces and their families.
  • The Royal British Legion: Providing lifelong support for the Royal Navy, British Army, Royal Air Force, Reservists, veterans, and their families.
  • Veterans UK: Administers the armed forces pension schemes and compensation payments for those injured or bereaved through service.
  • War Widows Association Of Great Britain: A pressure group and exists to improve the conditions of War Widows and their dependants in GB.
  • Charity Commission: We register and regulate charities in England and Wales, to ensure that the public can support charities with confidence.
